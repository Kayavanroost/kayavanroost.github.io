Postdoctoral fellow in epidemiology

I am a post-doctoral fellow in epidemiology at the Urban Health Collaborative at the Drexel Dornsife School of Public Health. My primary interest is in legal epidemiology, or the study of how laws and policies influence population-level health. I often quasi-experimental methods to evaluate the health impacts of various types of policies. I also work on a number of topics in social epidemiology, especially those related to adolescent sexual and reproductive health.

Research Curriculum Vitae

About

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow at the Drexel Dornsife School of Public Health. My research examines how laws, institutions, and public policy influence health equity. I combine policy surveillance, epidemiologic methods, and causal inference to evaluate the health impacts of public policy across diverse domains including reproductive health and correctional health.
